URBAN PLANNING

Title: New urban planning for the village of Smęgorzyno
Location: Smęgorzyno, Poland


Introducing a fresh layout and village center, design aims to cultivate a community that prioritizes residents' well-being. This eco-friendly development not only includes educational facilities but also promotes sustainable tourism, agriculture, and showcases the natural beauty of the landscape.
