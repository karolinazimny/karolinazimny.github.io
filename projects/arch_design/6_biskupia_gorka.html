URBAN DESIGN

Title: Revitalization of degraded urban tissue in Biskupia Górka
Location: Gdańsk, Poland


Revitalization of the entire area, including historic tenement houses. Introduction of new elements of small architecture, such as a cinema and open-air theater, and new playing fields. Proposing to build a cable car to the top of the mountain and dig a tunnel connecting the two parts of the city.
